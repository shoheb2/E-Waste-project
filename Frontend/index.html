ABOUT ECONOVA

ECONOVA was founded in 2023 with a vision to transform waste management through innovative and sustainable practices. Our mission is to provide seamless waste management solutions that prioritize sustainability and environmental responsibility. We are dedicated to reducing landfill waste and promoting recycling through our user-friendly platform. Our core values include Sustainability, Transparency, and Community, which guide our operations and interactions. Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, greener future as we engage and educate our community about responsible waste management.
